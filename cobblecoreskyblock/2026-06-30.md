# Added

- New **Void Mining** skill — mine in the void beneath your island for rare ores and gems
- Island Upgrades system — spend Island Points to unlock larger borders, more hoppers, and extra member slots
- **Cobblestone Generator Tiers** — upgrade your generator from stone all the way to Ancient Debris
- New **/is warp** public warp system — let other players visit your island
- Seasonal **Fishing Tournament** every Saturday at 3PM EST with trophy rewards
- 6 new crop types added to the farming skill: Starfruit, Moonbloom, Glacial Wheat, and more
- **Island Bank** — store money directly on your island, shared between all members
- New Minion type: **Lumberjack Minion** — automatically chops and replants your island trees
- Challenge system — complete island milestones for bonus Island Points and cosmetics
- **/trade** command for safe face-to-face player trading

# Changed

- Minion speed formula reworked — higher tier minions are now significantly faster
- Auction House listing fee reduced from 5% to 2%
- Island level calculation now accounts for rare blocks (Emerald, Diamond, Netherite)
- Slayer quest cooldown reduced from 24 hours to 12 hours
- Bazaar now supports buy orders up to 30 days instead of 7
- XP bottles from challenges now scale with your current skill level
- Revamped the **/shop** layout — categories are easier to navigate

# Fixed

- Fixed minions occasionally stopping when the island owner logged off
- Fixed **/is kick** not removing a player from the island member list correctly
- Fixed crop growth being blocked by certain light level configurations
- Fixed the Auction House showing sold listings as still active
- Fixed island border not updating visually after an upgrade was purchased
- Fixed Slayer boss not spawning if the player moved too far from the summon location
- Fixed **/trade** sometimes duplicating items on a failed transaction

# Removed

- Removed the old **/visit** command — replaced by the new **/is warp** system
- Removed Tier 1 starter kit — replaced by the new island tutorial reward track
