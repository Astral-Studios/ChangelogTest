*Note this is just a test changelog.*

# Added

- New **Warzone** PvP region with dynamic loot crates that drop every 30 minutes
- Faction Shields — protect your base from raids for up to 6 hours using Shield Charges
- **/f bounty** command — place a bounty on enemy faction members using in-game currency
- New **Obsidian Breaker** enchantment available through the Black Market
- Seasonal Battle Pass with 30 tiers of cosmetic rewards and exclusive titles
- Grace period system for newly created factions (48 hours of raid protection)
- **/f top** leaderboard now tracks kills, value, and raid score separately

# Changed

- Overhauled the economy — crop prices rebalanced to reduce AFK farming income
- Faction power system reworked; power now decays at 1 per hour when offline
- Cane and cactus farm limits reduced from 256 to 128 blocks per chunk
- KOTH capture time increased from 5 to 10 minutes
- TNT fill rate reduced by 20% in all warzone border chunks
- Black Market now rotates stock every 12 hours instead of 24

# Fixed

- Fixed an exploit allowing players to bypass faction territory using Ender Pearls
- Fixed shop signs not updating prices after a server restart
- Fixed **/f map** rendering incorrectly for factions with long names
- Fixed faction chat leaking into global chat when rejoining the server
- Fixed a dupe involving shulker boxes and the faction vault
- Fixed bounties not paying out when a target was killed in the warzone

# Removed

- Removed the old Crates system — replaced by the new Battle Pass reward track
- Removed **/wild** command from Warzone border chunks to prevent safezone abuse
